    Frequently Asked Questions

    What will be the work profile of the caretaker? +

    The caretaker assists with daily activities such as bathing, grooming, feeding, mobility support, medication reminders, and basic health monitoring. Responsibilities are customized based on patient needs.

    How can we verify that the caregiver is from HealthCare24? +

    All caregivers carry a verified HealthCare24 ID card and wear professional uniforms. Caregiver details are shared with the family before service begins.

    Are the caregivers trained and background verified? +

    Yes. All caregivers undergo background checks, medical screening, and structured training before deployment. Continuous assessments ensure service quality.

    How and when can I request a replacement? +

    Replacement requests are processed immediately. In most cases, a new caregiver is arranged within 24 hours, depending on availability.

    Can I meet or speak with the caregiver before confirmation? +

    Yes, you may interact with the caregiver either in person or via call before confirming the service to ensure comfort and compatibility.

    What kind of support is available after service starts? +

    Dedicated care coordinators remain available throughout the service period. You can reach support for feedback, emergencies, or assistance at any time.

    Is Care24 available in multiple cities? +

    Yes, HealthCare24 services are available across major cities including Mumbai, Navi Mumbai , Thane , and surrounding regions. Availability may vary by service type.
